# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## Company Overview

Welcome to Acme Corp! This handbook outlines our key policies and procedures. Please read it carefully and reach out to HR with any questions.

---

## Vacation Policy

### Annual Allowance

- **0-2 years of service**: 15 days paid vacation per year
- **3-5 years of service**: 20 days paid vacation per year
- **6+ years of service**: 25 days paid vacation per year

### Requesting Time Off

1. Submit vacation requests through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Requests of 5+ consecutive days require manager approval
3. December holidays require 4 weeks advance notice due to high demand

### Carryover Policy

- Up to 5 unused vacation days may be carried over to the next calendar year
- Carried-over days must be used by March 31st
- Unused days beyond the carryover limit will be forfeited

---

## Sick Leave Policy

### Annual Allowance

All employees receive 10 paid sick days per calendar year, available from your start date.

### Usage Guidelines

- Sick leave may be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or caring for immediate family members
- For absences of 3+ consecutive days, a doctor's note is required
- Unused sick days do not carry over and are not paid out upon termination

### Reporting Absences

- Notify your manager before your scheduled start time via email or phone
- Log sick days in the HR portal within 48 hours of returning to work
- Extended illness (5+ days) requires coordination with HR for potential short-term disability

## Remote Work Policy

### Eligibility

Remote work is available to employees who:
- Have completed their 90-day probationary period
- Maintain satisfactory performance ratings
- Have manager approval

### Hybrid Schedule

- Standard arrangement: 3 days in-office, 2 days remote per week
- Core in-office days are Tuesday and Thursday for team collaboration
- Fully remote positions are available for certain roles with VP approval

### Requirements

- Reliable high-speed internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Availability during core hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM local time
- Use of company VPN for all work-related activities

### Equipment

- Company provides laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse for remote work
- $500 one-time stipend for home office setup (desk, chair, etc.)
- IT support available remotely via helpdesk portal

## Expense Policy

### Eligible Expenses

The following business expenses are reimbursable:

| Category | Limit | Approval Required |
|----------|-------|-------------------|
| Meals (client meetings) | $75/person | Manager |
| Travel (flights, hotels) | Reasonable cost | Manager + Finance |
| Office supplies | $100/month | None |
| Professional development | $2,000/year | Manager |
| Software subscriptions | $50/month | IT + Manager |

### Submission Process

1. Submit expense reports within 30 days of incurring the expense
2. Include itemized receipts for all expenses over $25
3. Use the Expensify app linked to your company email
4. Reimbursements are processed bi-weekly on payroll dates

### Travel Guidelines

- Book flights at least 14 days in advance when possible
- Economy class required for flights under 6 hours
- Hotel rates should not exceed $200/night in major cities
- Rental cars require pre-approval; use preferred vendors for discounts
- Mileage reimbursement: $0.67 per mile for personal vehicle use

### Non-Reimbursable Expenses

- Alcoholic beverages (except at approved client events)
- Personal entertainment
- Traffic violations or parking tickets
- Airline upgrades without prior approval
- Expenses for family members or non-employees
